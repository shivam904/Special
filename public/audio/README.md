# Audio Files for "When I Think of You"

Place your audio files here for section-specific music.

## Required Files

Each section can have its own music track:

| File | Section | Suggestion |
|------|---------|------------|
| `opening.mp3` | Opening ("When I think of you...") | Soft piano intro |
| `section1.mp3` | Photo 1 (Sunlight smile) | Gentle acoustic |
| `section2.mp3` | Photo 2 (Glowing) | Warm strings |
| `section3.mp3` | Photo 3 (Cool pose) | Light upbeat |
| `section4.mp3` | Photo 4 (Mountains) | Nature ambience |
| `section5.mp3` | Photo 5 (Black dress) | Elegant piano |
| `section6.mp3` | Photo 6 (Casual) | Chill lo-fi |
| `section7.mp3` | Photo 7 (Traditional) | Indian classical fusion |
| `section8.mp3` | Photo 8 (Cozy) | Warm acoustic |
| `closing.mp3` | Closing (Honesty) | Soft fade out |
| `ambient.mp3` | Fallback/global | Gentle ambient |

## Tips

- Keep audio files under 2MB each for fast loading
- Use MP3 format (128-256kbps)
- Choose music that's loopable (seamless)
- Keep volume low - the config sets 35% max
- Emotional, not dramatic

## Royalty-Free Music Sources

- [Pixabay Music](https://pixabay.com/music/)
- [Free Music Archive](https://freemusicarchive.org/)
- [Mixkit](https://mixkit.co/free-stock-music/)
- [Bensound](https://www.bensound.com/)

## Quick Option

If you want ONE track for everything, just add `ambient.mp3` and remove the `music` property from each section in `src/config/content.js`.
